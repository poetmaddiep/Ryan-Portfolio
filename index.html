About Ryan

Hailing from Kalamazoo, Michigan is Ryan Rutkoskie. Since discovering poetry slams in Michigan, Ryan Rutkoskie has been combining the the energy of slam performance with the influence of writers he was exposed to early in life like Sylvia Plath and Philip Levine.

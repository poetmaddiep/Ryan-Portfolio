About Me

Ryan Rutkoskie is a creative visionary with a flair for fashion and storytelling. Born in Kalamazoo, Michigan, his poetry delves into the intricate intersections of capitalism's pitfalls and the raw, unfiltered nuances of romantic intimacy. Through his evocative words, he invites readers to navigate the complexities of human emotion and societal critique. A true artisan, Ryan crafts each poem as a masterpiece of vulnerability and resilience.
